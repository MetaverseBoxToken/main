Why choose us?

We studied 80% of the game models on the market and none of them could solve the problem of overcapacity in blockchain games (output > consumption). So the METABOX token was born. In order to solve all the difficulty in the game, NFT mints with $METABOX tokens and the amount of tokens consumed is determined by the oracle.

Metaverse Box (METABOX) is no ordinary gamefi. To solve the problem of overcapacity, we made a bold decision. In the world of the meta box, the games will not generate any token revenue. Of course, this is not absolute, as we will hold game competitions to reward players who win points. There is a 5% token transaction tax to reward players.
